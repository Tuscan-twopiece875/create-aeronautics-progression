## 🎮 Setting up the game

Follow these steps to integrate the mod into your local Minecraft installation:

1.  Open the folder you just extracted.
2.  Locate your Minecraft installation directory on your PC. This is typically found at `%appdata%\.minecraft`.
3.  Ensure you have NeoForge installed. Use the version specified in the mod documentation folder.
4.  Copy the files from the downloaded folder into the `mods` folder inside your Minecraft directory.
5.  Launch the Minecraft Launcher.
6.  Select the NeoForge profile from the bottom left menu.
7.  Click Play.

If the game crashes, verify that your Java version matches the requirement. Remove other mods that might cause conflicts with aeronautics systems.

## 📘 Learning the mechanics

This project breaks down the mod into four distinct stages. Follow this order to ensure you understand every machine before moving to complex flight systems.

### ⚙️ Stage 1: Basic engines
Start by gathering copper and iron. You must build a basic rotational engine to provide power. Align your shafts and gearboxes carefully. Even a small error in alignment stops the flow of power to your machines.

### 🏗️ Stage 2: Structural integrity
Large ships require proper frame design. Learn how to connect chassis blocks to form the main frame. Keep your block count in check. Too many blocks overload your engine capacity.

### ☁️ Stage 3: Flight controls
Install the helm and control surfaces. You use these to steer and pitch your craft. Connect the helm to your rotational engines using redstone links. Test your ship on a small lake before attempting high-altitude flight.

### 🚀 Stage 4: Advanced propulsion
Once you master flight, add turbochargers and fuel injectors. These parts grant higher speeds and better stability. Monitor your heat levels via the gauge on your control panel. High heat might damage your engine components.

## 🔍 Troubleshooting common issues

### The ship does not move
Check if your ship contains disconnected blocks. The game expects a unified structure. Use the "Goggles" item to highlight connected parts. Ensure your fuel tank has enough internal storage for the journey.

### The game runs slowly
Lower your chunk distance in the video settings. Aeronautics mod generates large amounts of data for ship movement. Reduce the number of active mechanical entities if your computer struggles to maintain a stable frame rate.

### Missing textures
Verify your resource pack settings. If you use custom packs, they might lack the files for the new 2026 machines. Turn off custom resource packs to see if the default textures appear correctly.
